What is HERO?

HERO(Conjunction of Heroes) is a community driven to bring players from around the globe the most enjoyable experience from video games. Using Discord, we communicate on a daily basis from a wide range of topics, including: Video Games, Movies, TV Shows, Politics, etc. We play games such as: Guild Wars 2, Dota 2, Starcraft II, Fortnite, and more!
